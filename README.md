# Alexandria 🔍📚
A CLI for easily browsing your personal local story collection!

Tired of sifting through pdfs, epubs etc when trying to find something to read in your personal collection?
With Alexandria:
- Easily add summaries, notes, ratings and tags to your stories and view them in a readable, pretty format!
- With your personal tags you can search for stories with your specific vision (romance, absolute anguish)
- Create collections, stories can belong to multiple collections
- And more to come...
